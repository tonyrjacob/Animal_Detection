About Us

Welcome to our animal detection application powered by YOLOv7. Our mission is to provide a user-friendly platform that allows users to detect and identify various animal species using advanced computer vision technology.

With a passion for technology, our team has worked tirelessly to create this application, aiming to raise awareness about the diverse animal kingdom and promote the importance of coexisting with nature.

Our Team

We are a dedicated team of developers, designers, and wildlife enthusiasts who joined forces to create this animal detection application. Each member brings a unique set of skills and expertise, contributing to the success of our project.
